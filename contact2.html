Brian Degger

Hello, I am Brian Degger, a maker with a PhD in biotechnology and an interest in open hardware, software and DIYBio. I specialise in the more unusual projects often working with startups and artists.

For the last ten years I have been working with artists and participating in workshops and residencies.

I am also a founding member of the Maker Space in Newcastle upon Tyne, an active member of the North East Maker community, and have a background in biology.
