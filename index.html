Brian Degger

Hello, I am Dr Brian Degger, a maker with a PhD in biotechnology and an passion for open hardware, software and science. I specialise in the more unusual projects often working with artists.

For the last ten years I have been working with artists, participating in workshops and residencies around the world amd developing my own technoscientific art practice.

I am also a founding member of the Maker Space in Newcastle upon Tyne, an active member of the North East Maker community.

I am embarking on a PGCE in Biology this year (2018).
